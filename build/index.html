Welcome to the amazing Battleship game!

As I started to learn JavaScript, I decided to make this game as the practice of things I learned. This is an amazing Battleship game where you choose the place of 4 ships on the 5x5 field. After both players choose the positions of ships, in each turn you will guess the potential place of the enemy ship. The one who first finds 4 enemy ships is a winner. Good luck!
